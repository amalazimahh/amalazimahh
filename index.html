6 LOCAL DELICACIES YOU MUST TRY!

AMBUYAT

Brunei's national cuisine. It is made from the interior of a sago palm tree. Has a mixture of water and white solids. It is served with dip 'cacah' (sauce), that tastes both spicy and sour. Ambuyat is edible without chewing it. You just have to swallow it to taste after the savoury.

NASI KATOK

Nasi means rice, while Katok means knock. The history behind the name of this dish is, back then, people would usually have to knock on seller's door to buy the dish and it happened to stuck to this day. It is the best dish of all time. The must have contents of the dish is one serving of rice, one serving of chicken and dip of sambal.

KELUPIS

It is one of the best snacks to the country. Made from a glutinous rice. Interestingly, it is wrapped with Nyirik leaf. For some people who prefer to get experimental with the taste of kelupis, they would usually served it with anchovies and some would go for dried shrimp.

PULUT PANGGANG

This is another famous snacks in the country as it usually run out quickly as early as in the afternoon. The contents of pulut panggang is similar to Kelupis, except that it is wrapped and grilled instead of steamed.

KUIH BAHULU

Kuih Bahulu that consists of a sponge cake made in a cast iron mold.

BAMBOO CHICKEN

An aromantic dish that has been a secret recipe to the Iban longhouse communities. The preparation of the dish is by marinating chicken and stuffing it into bamboo poles with other spices like onions. It is then wedged shut with bamboo leaves. It is then nestled on top of the fire, filling the air with the sweet aroma as it slowly cooks to perfection. If you eat this with rice, it automatically becomes your favorite dish in Brunei.

Explore The Nature In Brunei

A visit to Brunei means that it is crucial to have a trip to nature and wildlife destination !
Here are some suggested destination for you to visit :

BUKIT LUTUT

Bukit Lutut is a perfect destination for people who loves camping. Whether you are coming with friends or family, you wouldn't want to miss the beautiful scene of sunrise in the morning after a very good night sleep of camping and to wake up to peaceful sound of birds chirping.

BELALONG CANOPY WALKWAY

An adventurous place where you must climb up about 726 stairs to reach to the grand structure of about 43 meters tall ! Walk through the walkway surrounded by tall canopies that will leave you an unforgettable experience!

ULU-ULU RESORT

At Ulu-Ulu Resort, green practices and social responsibilty are implemented such as using water from the river itself where the water is filtered and non-chlorinated as well as low energy to conserve energy and to make use of green practices. Have a walk during night to make it an unforgettable moment of complete peace.

SUMBILING ECO VILLAGE

It is known to be untouched breathtaking nature as it is completely different from the city of Brunei. Enhance your experience of local culture by doing some activities offered at Sumbiling Eco Village. The activities include traditional dance and music, cooking session, river tubing, night walk, sunrise walk, rock painting and many more !

Get To Know About Brunei's Culture And Heritage

BALAI PAMERAN SULTAN HAJI HASSANAL BOLKIAH

Love going to the art gallery and museums?

A visit to Balai Pameran Sultan Haji Hassanal Bolkiah will give you more insight of the culture. It is an Islamic Heritage Museum with impressive architecture, has nine galleries which displays priceless islamic artifacts of His Majesty's personal collection. Whether you want to learn more about Islam or to see new things about Islam in Brunei, this is the perfect destination for you.

ROYAL REGALIA MUSEUM

A museum which displays grand trappings of His Majesty and royalty.

Royal Regalia Museum gives you an insight of during the childhood days of His Majesty to the coronation day. A film footage of the auspicious event can also be view. The exhibition gallery showcases jewel-crusted crowns, thrones, revered silver Keris and many more of His Majesty'

KAMPONG AYER

Also known as 'Venice of the East'

The houses are built on stilts above the river, most of the traditional lifestyle remained unchanged such as fisherman and trader. Interestingly, people have been living here for more than 1,300 years ! Take one of the many water taxis in front of the Yayasan Sultan Haji Hassanal Bolkiah and experience the heritage found here !

JAME' ASR HASSANIL BOLKIAH MOSQUE

Another fine example of decorated islamic architecture.

This mosque is surrounded with garden and fountain, has 29 pointed golden domes which represents His Majesty's 29th dynasty. It is built in 1994 and is built with full devotion of details. A visit during the night will give you the cool breeze and serenity, rejuvenate your soul and mind. Moreover, the view of this mosque at night is amazingly beautiful!
